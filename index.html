Terr-a-spect

Capturing the hidden beauty of nature · Wildlife · Macro · Landscapes

Terr-a-spect is a nature content studio. We produce original photography and short-form video of the natural world and publish it under the Terr-a-spect brand across social platforms — TikTok, Facebook, Instagram, YouTube and Telegram.

Original content

Every photo, video and soundtrack is produced in-house. We publish only material we create and own.

Scheduled publishing

Our publishing tool prepares captions and uploads finished videos to our own connected brand accounts at planned times.

Official APIs only

Content is delivered through each platform's official Content Posting API using a secure OAuth connection to our own accounts.

How the TikTok integration works

The Terr-a-spect publishing tool connects to our own TikTok account through TikTok's official OAuth flow and uses the Content Posting API to upload and publish our own finished nature videos. It reads only the basic information of the connected account to confirm the right account is linked. It does not access, collect or post content on behalf of any other TikTok users.
