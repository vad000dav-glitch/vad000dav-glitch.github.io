Transparency

How our integrations use your data

The Terr-a-spect publishing tool connects only to our own brand accounts through each platform's official OAuth flow, in order to upload and publish our own finished content.

  • It stores only the authorization tokens for our own connected accounts, kept privately on our own secured server.
  • It reads basic account info (identifier and display name) only to confirm the right account is connected.
  • It does not access, collect or post content on behalf of any other users, and never sells or shares data.

Data deletion: to request removal of any data the tool holds, email vad000dav@gmail.com. Authorization can also be revoked at any time from the connected account's settings, which immediately stops all access.
