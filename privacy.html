Privacy Policy — Terr-a-spect

Last updated: 15 June 2026

Terr-a-spect ("the App", "we", "us") is a private automation tool that publishes our own original nature photography and video content to our own brand accounts on social platforms, including TikTok, Facebook, Instagram, YouTube and Telegram. This Privacy Policy explains what data the App handles when it connects to TikTok.

1. Who this App is for

The App is operated by the owner of the Terr-a-spect channel for the sole purpose of posting the owner's own content to the owner's own connected accounts. It is not a multi-user service and does not act on behalf of other TikTok users.

2. What data we access

  • TikTok account authorization tokens (OAuth access and refresh tokens) for the single account that the owner connects, used only to upload and publish videos to that account.
  • Basic account information (user identifier, display name) returned by the user.info.basic scope, used only to confirm the correct account is connected.
  • Video files and captions that we ourselves create and submit for publishing.

3. What we do NOT do

  • We do not collect, store or process data of any other TikTok users.
  • We do not sell, rent or share any data with third parties for advertising or any other purpose.
  • We do not use the data for profiling, tracking or analytics about individuals.

4. How data is stored

Authorization tokens are stored privately on the owner's own server and are never exposed publicly. Tokens are used only to call TikTok's official Content Posting API. They can be revoked at any time from the connected TikTok account settings, which immediately stops the App's access.

5. Data retention

Tokens are kept only as long as the App is in use and are deleted when access is revoked or the integration is removed. Published content is hosted by TikTok under TikTok's own policies.

6. Third-party services

The App interacts with the official TikTok APIs. Use of TikTok is also governed by TikTok's Privacy Policy.
